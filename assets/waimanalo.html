Waimanalo

Waimanalo is located on the very south end of the Windward side of the island, and the second wettest of the weather stations analyzed. Here the average amount of rainfall was 0.12 inches and a max of 6.38 inches in one day. However, this weather does create some of the most breathtaking mountains and scenery anywhere in the world. The wind and water also play a factor on the beaches. With the wind and rain, the sand on this side of the island is the very fine powder white sand, which makes for a lovely day on the beach.
