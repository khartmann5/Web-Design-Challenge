Precipitation in Pearl City

Weather in Pearl City is dry compared to some of the other parts of the island. Pearl City is located on the South side of Oahu near Honolulu and the airport. Pearl City is known for being the home of Pearl Harbor. Here you can experience an in depth tour of the military machines used during World War II and see some of the famous sites such as the Arizona Memorial where oil is still leaking from the craft.

The precipitation is Pearl City is mostly dry during the summer months with some peaks up to 2+ inches of rain during the winter. The average amount of rainfall during the period of data was 0.063 inches with a max rainfall in one day of 2.8 inches.
