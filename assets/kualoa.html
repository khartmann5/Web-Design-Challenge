Precipitation in Kualoa

Weather in Kualoa is quite wet. Kualoa is located on the Windward side (east side) of Oahu where the island sees more rain than the other sides of the island. True to it's name of the Windward side this area of the island also experiences a lot of wind and creates some of the most beautiful landscapes in the world. The wind and water carve out the side of the mountains creating these steep cliffs covered in vegetation.

This area is where some of the movie Jurassic Park was filmed. The ranch near here is a great tourist destination where people can ride ATVs to see some of the locations used for films and is great for a concert venue with Chinamans Hat in the background.

According to the data presented the summer months appear to be the best time to visit with the least amount of precipitation, however the area does tend to get more rain than the other parts of the island with an average precipitation of 0.12 inches over the period of data. The max amount of rainfall in one day was 11.53 inches.
